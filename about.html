About

My Story

Born in Poland, grown in Copenhagen, shaped in Berlin and regularly asked how to pronounce my name.

My career path has not been exactly linear, which allows me to bring diverse experiences and perspectives into my work.

I hold a Master’s degree in language but gained professional experience in the tech and e-commerce industries.

In my previous roles I was highly recognized for my ability to manage (multiple) projects and tasks to deadline, as well my ability to swiftly adapt to changing timelines or expectations.

In my work, communication has always been tremendously important. As a team of one, nearly single-handedly serving the whole company’s visual content needs, regular connecting with other teams helped me ensure efforts are aligned and resources distributed efficiently.

My favorite work project I take a lot of pride in was creating a digital asset management automated solution enabling key stakeholders to self-access multiple visual asset libraries based on their content needs and legal requirements. This has enabled smooth cross-team collaboration on existing assets and significantly reduced the amount of time spent on imagery sourcing tasks and legal risks resulting from misinterpreting existing licensing constraints.

When I’m done solving the world's problems, you will find me crafting historical costumes and going back in time to produce images for museums and historical publications.

Having successfully completed a UX Design Education with a User Interface Design specialization at Careerfoundry, I am eager to combine my creative, visual skills with newly acquired design process tools & knowledge.

I am looking forward to devising innovative digital products in the tech or medical industry. While I am currently based in Berlin, I am open to relocate or to work remotely.

Feel free to reach out or contact me through my portfolio site: https://www.behance.net/fotoforge. There you can also find all of my latest work.
