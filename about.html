My story

My name is Kasia and I’m a UX/UI designer with a background in brand marketing and customer experience.

In my previous roles in fast-paced unicorn startups, I was highly recognized for my ability to manage (multiple) projects and tasks to deadline, as well my ability to swiftly adapt to changing timelines or expectations.

Being part of the team working on marketing projects with such brands as EasyJet, Merlin or Louvre, while nearly single-handedly serving the whole company’s visual content needs, regular connecting with other teams and setting up organizational solutions helped me ensure efforts are aligned and resources distributed efficiently.

I believe there is more than one solution to every problem. My passion for solving problems and simplifying things is what made me choose the path of user experience design.

Having successfully completed a UX Design Education with a User Interface Design and Front-End for Designers specialization at Careerfoundry, I am eager to combine my creative visual skills with newly acquired design process tools & knowledge.

I am looking forward to devising innovative digital products in the tech or medical industry. While I am currently based in Berlin, I am open to working remotely.
